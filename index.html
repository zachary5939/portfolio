Amazing: Shopping App

JavaScript, React, Redux, Express, Sequelize, AWS S3

"Amazing" is a shopping application inspired by Amazon, built to replicate some of the platform's essential features. Users can browse products and add them to their cart. Adjusting the cart is easy, making the shopping process flexible and user-friendly.

Once an order is placed, users have a 5-minute window to make any changes before the product is shipped. This ensures users have ample time to revise their decisions if needed.

Product reviews are an essential part of the platform. To ensure the reliability of feedback, only users who've purchased a product can review it. Additionally, a wishlist feature is available, allowing users to save products they might be interested in for later.

MeetHere: My first Full Stack App

JavaScript, React, Redux, Express, Sequelize

"MeetHere" is basic clone of MeetUp. "MeetHere" is a platform that allows users to create and join groups based on shared interests. It's inspired by MeetUp and aims to bring people together around common passions or activities.

Group owners have the privilege of creating events for their members, ensuring that every gathering is tailored to the group's focus. For those who aren't group owners, they can easily join and view upcoming events but won't have management capabilities for those events.

This design ensures a structured approach where group creators have control over the events linked to their groups, enhancing the experience for all members. Whether you're looking to view group events or create your own, "MeetHere" offers a seamless way to connect with like-minded individuals.

Toontown Content Packs

Toontown Content Packs

Panda3D, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Toontown Content Packs offer players a refreshed gaming experience by reimagining the aesthetics of the beloved game Toontown. These packs, serving as texture enhancements, transform both visuals and sounds to offer an entirely new perspective of Toontown.

Built using Panda3D, these Content Packs are essentially multifiles that, when placed into specific game directories, override the default Toontown assets. This alteration allows players to experience customized visuals and audios, ranging from retextured in-game elements to revamped background scores.

At the age of 14, using a template, I ventured into web development by creating contentpacks.net. While initially designed to host the Content Packs I crafted during my younger years, the site eventually evolved into a hub showcasing the diverse offerings of the Toontown Community. Though I haven't updated it since completing the Full Stack Software Engineering course at App Academy, I remain immensely proud of what contentpacks.net represents.

contentpacks.net proudly stands as the go-to hub for these transformative packs, hosting and offering them at no cost to the community. Since its inception, the site has witnessed over 590,000 Content Pack downloads and continues to attract approximately 8,500 unique visitors every month.

Toontown Rewritten

Panda3D, Notion, Communication, Constructive Feedback, Python

Toontown Rewritten stands as a nostalgic yet rejuvenated beacon for Disney's Toontown Online enthusiasts. Our revival of the beloved game has now spanned a longer timeframe than the original, which Disney ceased operations on. Our commitment and passion for Toontown are evident in our efforts to keep its spirit alive and thriving.

One of the most notable contributions to this legacy is the "Sellbot Task Force" update. This expansion is the most substantial addition to Toontown since 2008, offering over 50 hours of original content. Every element and feature of this expansion was conceived, designed, and implemented from scratch by our dedicated team. We strive to craft new content that meets and exceeds industry benchmarks in gameplay quality and stringent moderation standards.

As the Quality Assurance Director for the Toontown Remastered project, I lead the initiative to remaster nearly every in-game asset originally created in the 2000s. This involves overseeing the entire quality assurance process to ensure that each remastered asset meets our high standards and significantly enhances the overall player experience. My leadership in this role ensures that our team maintains a meticulous approach to quality and consistency across all assets.

I am also responsible for meticulously documenting and reporting bugs on Notion with reproducible steps to aid our game developers in resolving them swiftly. I also analyzed game logs for potential crash culprits. My responsibilities extended beyond bug reports to include effective communication with diverse departments and offering constructive feedback, all in pursuit of a seamless and immersive gaming experience for our community.
